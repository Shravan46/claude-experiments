# Smart Vibration "Health Patch" for Industrial Machines

## The Concept
A small, adhesive wireless sensor patch that attaches directly to any machine surface — like a bandage — and continuously monitors **vibration signatures, temperature, and acoustic patterns** to detect early signs of mechanical failure.

## What Makes It Different

**Self-learning baseline** — It learns each specific machine's normal "heartbeat" during the first week, so alerts are tailored rather than generic.

**Plain-language alerts** — Instead of raw data, it sends messages like:
> *"Bearing on Conveyor 3 showing unusual wear — estimated 2 weeks before failure"*

**No rewiring required** — Purely adhesive and wireless, so maintenance teams can deploy dozens in an afternoon without an engineer.

## Key Features
- 6-month battery life
- Integrates with existing maintenance software or a simple phone app
- Color-coded LED visible from the factory floor (green/yellow/red)
- Disposable or rechargeable options

## The Value
- Catches problems **weeks before breakdown**
- Reduces reliance on scheduled maintenance that may be too early or too late
- Affordable enough to put on *every* machine, not just critical ones

---

The core idea is making predictive maintenance **as simple as putting on a bandage** — removing the biggest barrier, which is complexity of installation.
